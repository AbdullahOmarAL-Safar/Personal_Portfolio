About Me

My name is Abdullah Omar Akram AL-Safar, a passionate Full Stack Developer and a YouTube content creator. My journey in tech started with the goal of sharing knowledge in a simple, accessible way, especially for Arabic-speaking learners.

Through my brand LEVEL UP IT, I’ve taught programming topics using clear, beginner-friendly tutorials. I’ve launched two active YouTube channels, gaining over 42K+ views across videos, and continue to publish content that supports and motivates future developers.

I have hands-on experience with technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Tailwind and have deployed projects using GitHub. I'm also preparing for the GitHub Foundations Certification.

I believe in continuous learning, real-world projects, and building a solid foundation — which is why I’ve completed 12+ projects including UI components, calculators, clocks, API apps, and more.

My mission is to make tech education simpler, accessible, and more enjoyable for everyone — especially in the Arab world.
